    J3U Pro-Mindset

    Stimulus is King (SFR)

    Your goal is the highest muscle stimulus with the least joint/systemic fatigue. Choose exercises you FEEL perfectly. "Good form" is form that works for YOUR body.

    The 3 Tiers of Fatigue

    1. Muscle Soreness (Good): The sign of effective stimulus.
    2. Connective Tissue Stress (Warning): Nagging, achy joints. Swap the exercise.
    3. Systemic/CNS Fatigue (Red Alert): Low motivation, bad sleep, strength drops. Time for a deload.

    The Pro's Logbook

    Log numbers, but also log the FEEL. "10 perfect reps" is a bigger win than "12 sloppy reps." Progressive overload must be sustainable.
